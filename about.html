About

Marlene is a cartoonist and illustrator living in New York City. As an artist she works with a variety of different topics, usually resulting in pleasent but deep narratives. Her work has been featured by entities such as The Yetee and Tapas Media.

She is a member of the Scope of Work 2024 cohort and Cartoonist Cooperative. She is open to exploring new opportunities in order to better improve her craft.
